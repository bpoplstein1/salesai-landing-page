How it works

A fixed-scope install.
Three phases, 6 to 8 weeks.

Embedded part-time with your team, built into your real tools and your real work, so the knowledge transfers as we go. One clear success metric. Fixed scope, fixed price.

Phase 1Week 1

Diagnose

We map your real workflows and the systems you run your business on, find the highest-leverage loops to automate, and define one clear success metric. You get a prioritized plan before we build anything.

Phase 2Weeks 2–6

Build

We stand up the brain (your source of truth), build 1 to 2 agent workflows into production, wire the orchestration layer across your systems, and put the guardrails in place behind human-in-the-loop gates. Built with your people, not in a vacuum.

Phase 3Weeks 7–8

Transfer

We train your team to use it, keep the source of truth updated, and keep a human in the loop so agents never silently change anything canonical. We hand over the SOPs and the runbook, then I hand you the keys and get out of the way.
